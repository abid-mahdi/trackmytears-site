Multi-Modal Journaling

Log emotional moments with text or voice memos. Classify tears as happy or sad with customizable intensity levels.

Dashboard & Insights

Visual summaries of your emotional trends with charts, weekly recaps, and personalized insights to help you grow.

Voice Memos

Record how you feel in the moment. WhatsApp-style inline player with waveform visualization and seek controls.

Memories & Patterns

"On this day" flashbacks and recurring trigger detection help you identify emotional patterns over time.

Ambient Sounds

8 calming soundscapes including rain, ocean waves, and forest sounds to create a peaceful journaling environment.
